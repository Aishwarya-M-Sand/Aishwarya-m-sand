Our Ethical Responsibility

Aishwarya M Sand believes it is our ethical responsibility to ensure:

  • Provide qualitative supply of materials
  • Adherence to a strict health and safety policy
  • Raise awareness to reduce waste production
  • Conduct all affairs in an ethical, legal, socially, and environmentally responsible manner

Quarry Divisions

To sustain unhindered supply of processed quarry products to our clients, we have acquired multiple quarries across Karnataka. Our vast reserves of granite enable us to fulfill demand of any scale without delays.

We pride ourselves on using advanced equipment like Sandvik CJ 411 Primary Jaw Crusher, VSI, and Classifiers for M-sand. Additionally, we conduct muffle blasting with expert supervision to ensure safe operations.

Mine Planning & Operations

Our mine planning segment is led by experienced mining engineers and consultants who develop sustainable strategies using modern technology. We are proud to align with government regulations, ensuring all operations meet legal and environmental standards.

Our corporate social responsibility initiatives include supporting road development, aiding home construction for the financially downtrodden, and funding medical camps and charity activities in nearby communities.
